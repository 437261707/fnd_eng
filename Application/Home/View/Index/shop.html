AboutUs

Funengda is a high-tech enterprise in R&D, production, sales, and after-sales service. With a view to the global water purification, water health, water security, and water crisis, we have been committed to the scientific development of drinking water for the human beings. Jinan Funengda Water Technology Development Co.,Ltd. was founded in 1998. Shenzhen Funengda Air&Water Science Technology Development Co.,Ltd. was founded in 2013; And its headquarter locates in the innovation capital of China-Shenzhen.
We have three R&D bases: Shenzhen Funengda Air&Water Science Technology Development Co.,Ltd., Jinan Funengda Water Technology Development Co.,Ltd., and Guangdong Shunde Hongshu Electric Technology Co.,Ltd. Shenzhen Funengda Air&Water Science Technology Development Co.,Ltd. has made remarkable achievements in sewage treatment, and drinking water security for urban and rural residents. It has produced ‘the first business air-water generator in the world’, ‘the first zero-discharge water purifier in China’, and initiated a new revolution in water purification industry. Shenzhen Funengda Air&Water Science Technology Development Co.,Ltd. has acquired many patents in water technology. For instance, the zero-discharge water purifier technology, and high performance molecular filtration&modification technology are the innovation patents exclusive to the company. Funengda water technology products fall into 8 categories and over 100 types, and are widely applied to family, office, hotel, restaurant, ship, holiday flat, hospital, construction site, mine, marine drilling platform, naval vessel, livestock & poultry farm, and military. They can demonstrate their unique advantages especially in the natural disasters’ rescue scenes. With a concept of ‘Creating Tomorrow’s Water Source’, and a mission of ‘Producing Superior Water for the People Globally with the Least Cost’, the company has become an innovator and pioneer in air-water industry, and has dedicated to addressing the water issue in the world.
